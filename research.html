REDS Project

Completed

Smart Urban Electric Distribution for a Reliable and Efficient Electric Power Supply to all Consumers.

Funded by NAST Nepal
Duration: March 2024 - February 2025
RA-Interns: Ms. Anu Dhakal (Masters student), Mr. Rohan Thapa (Undergraduate student), Mr. Rupesh Neupane (Undergraduate student)
Output: 1 Conference presentation, 1 Journal publication
Executive Summary:
This study focuses on the development of a tool for outage management. Data from various field devices such as smart meters and automated devices will be analyzed and visualized to help electric system operators monitor voltage quality. An AI-based algorithm will utilize this data to identify precise electrical fault locations and provide correct procedures for power restoration. The tool aims to reduce frequent power outages and energy loss, ultimately contributing to better urban development.
