Welcome to the
Grid Modernization Lab

Advancing Nepal’s power grid with smart technologies, digital substations, and cutting-edge research in renewable energy integration, AI, and power systems.

Research & Objectives

Our lab develops advanced analysis tools and prototypes to strengthen the Integrated Nepal Power System.

Tool Development

We develop advanced analysis tools like D-VA Grid (Distview) with AI and probabilistic methods to ensure the grid is accurate, efficient, and robust.

Technology Demonstration

Demonstrating cutting-edge technologies for grid enhancement through real-world implementations, focusing on Digital Twins and Wide Area Monitoring.

Opportunities

Providing students and researchers with meaningful opportunities to participate in real-world projects, nurturing skills in energy systems and technology.

Collaboration Hub

Serving as a central hub for academicians and industrialists to collaborate on grid modernization, fostering trust between Industry and University.

Renewable Integration

Researching the application of storage (pumped hydro) and renewable resources to reduce import reliance and strengthen local generation.

Impact Analysis

Improving socio-economic conditions by increasing electricity reliability and creating sustainable income through overhead cost reduction and patents.
